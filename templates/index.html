Welcome to the Music Voting App!

Here, you can vote for the music you want to hear in the pub.

Start Voting

Get Started

To vote for your favorite music, simply scan the QR code provided by the pub with your phone's camera.

Look for the QR code on your table or ask a staff member for assistance if you can't find it.

Once scanned, you'll be directed to the voting page where you can choose the next song to play!
